Transport Layer Security (TLS)


TLS is a cryptographic protocol that provides end-to-end
communications security over networks and is widely used
for internet communications and online transactions.  It
is an IETF standard intended to prevent eavesdropping,
tampering and message forgery.  Nov 9, 2018
--www.networkworld.com/article/2303073/lan-wan-what-is-transport-layer-security-protocol.html
